About Us

Welcome to Sabaragamuwa University Medical Centre, your dedicated healthcare provider on campus.

Our mission is to ensure the well-being of students by offering accessible and reliable medical services. Located conveniently within the university premises, we provide basic medical care to address a range of health concerns.

With a team of skilled healthcare professionals, we strive to offer personalized care and support to all students. From minor illnesses to preventive care, we are here to serve you during our weekday hours from 8.00 a.m. to 4.00 p.m. In addition to our on-campus services, we collaborate closely with the Belihuloya divisional hospital, ensuring seamless access to specialized medical assistance when needed.

APPLICATION FOR MEDICAL EXAM OR LECTURE

Medical Request Instructions

  • Students must upload a PDF document confirmed by a government doctor.
  • All other fields must be correctly filled out. If not, the medical request will be rejected.
  • When filling out the "Exam or Lecture Details" section, follow this structure:
    • Name: [Exam/Lecture Name]
    • Subject: [Subject]
    • Subject code: [Subject code]
    • Exam/Lecture Date: [Exam/Lecture Date]
    • Medical Date: [Date of Medical Issue]
  • If a student requests medicals for more than one lecture or exam, they need to submit medical requests for each separately.

Help us to Maintain good Service

Donating medicine can be a lifesaving act that brings hope to those in need. Every donation contributes to the well-being of individuals who might otherwise struggle to access essential medications. Whether it's providing antibiotics to treat infections, insulin for diabetes management, or chemotherapy drugs for cancer patients, each donation has the potential to save lives and alleviate suffering. By giving medicine, we extend a lifeline to those facing health challenges, offering them not just treatment, but also a renewed chance at life. It's a powerful gesture of compassion and solidarity, demonstrating that together, we can make a tangible difference in the lives of others.
